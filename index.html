Ghost Fiddle

Web App controlled robotic glass harp

Ghost Fiddle

My teammates and I built a webapp controlled robotic glass hard. Our mechanical set-up involved spinning moist foam brushes over the rims of our 8 wine glasses. We controlled our motors using a Raspberry Pi that served a raphael.js based webapp which allowed users to input custom or preloaded songs and send them to our glass hap to be played.

Genetic Algorithm

A coevolving predator-prey simulation

Genetic Algorithms: Predator-Prey Simulation

This exploration of genetic algorithms began with a simple problem. A predator and prey are pitted against each other, the predator is faster but less agile and its goal is to catch the prey, while the prey's goal is to escape. Our goal is to find an ideal force vector function for each actor based on its own and the other's positions. In Python, a team of 3 and I constructed the pred-prey simulation and then applied a genetic algorithm to solve for these force vector functions.

Pipeline CPU

A 5-Stage MIPS compliant pipeline CPU built in Verilog

MIPS Pipeline CPU

Me and two teammates built a fully functioning MIPS compliant 5-stage pipeline CPU in Verilog. It featured data forwarding as well 2-bit branch prediction. We were able to successfully run user assembly code that made full use of our instruction set and hazard avoidance capabilities.
